nyari error antara koordinat posisi bola dan posisi yg  diinginkan :

error_x = ball_x - center_x

error_y = ball_y - center_y

*ball_x and ball_y are the coordinates of the center of the ball in the camera frame
*center_x and center_y are the coordinates of the center of the camera frame.


1. Proportional (P) Control :
=> adjust servo angle in proportion to the current error.
larger error -> larger servo angle adjustment

servo_angle_x = Kp_x * error_x

servo_angle_y = Kp_y * error_y

Kp_x and Kp_y are the proportional gain constants for the head-pan and head-tilt servos


2. Integral (I) Control :
=> adjust servo angle to reduce steady-state error.
It compensates for any biar or drift in the system

integral_x = integral_x + Ki_x * error_x

integral_y = integral_y + Ki_y * error_y

servo_angle_x = servo_angle_x + integral_x

servo_angle_y = servo_angle_y + integral_y

3. Ki_x and Ki_y are the integral gain constants for the head-pan and head-tilt servos
integral_x and integral_y are the cumulative error values.

Derivative (D) Control :
adjust servo angle based on the rate of change of the error.
it reduces overshoot and oscillation in the system

derivative_x = Kd_x * (error_x - prev_error_x)

derivative_y = Kd_y * (error_y - prev_error_y)

servo_angle_x = servo_angle_x + derivative_x

servo_angle_y = servo_angle_y + derivative_y

Kd_x and Kd_y are the derivative gain constants for the head-pan and head-tilt servos
prev_error_x and prev_error_y are the previous error values
